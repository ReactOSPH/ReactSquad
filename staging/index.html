A community-contributed operating system

Been in development since 1995, ReactOS has been proven that a free alternative to Windows can be achieved through passion of many!

Many feats and features have made ReactOS one of the best go-tos for an alternative OS that diversifies the hardware and software, among many things.

On its new age, ReactOS still remains a 'true' Windows alternative that can go leaps and bounds for the future of FLOSS and beyond.

Representing the Filipino and Italian audience

Founded since December 2021, ReactSquad aims to make ReactOS known in more communities than ever before, while also contributing to the development through its testing of the OS through hardware and software.

An idea that bore from the expertise of one, its goal is to unite the two communities while sharing the same experience for future generations.

Today, ReactSquad houses the Filipino and Italian Communities as an effort to make the contributions more known throughout the industry as a whole.
