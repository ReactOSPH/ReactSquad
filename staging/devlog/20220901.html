We have relaunched ReactSquad!

thebelovedmoon, Co-founder
1 Sep 2022


Following the near-end of the year and our upcoming first anniversary, we have evolved so much in presenting what's happening in the ReactOS Family of Communities! And following our launch of the website, we kept finding ways to connect to people and experience the open-source alternative to Windows. So with that, we've finally decided to relaunch the website slowly!

So, what's the deal?

We're constantly evolving! So with this, we're aiming to split our key areas into sections that will be easy for everyone, regardless of which locale. Most of the key areas are still existing, but there will be slow improvements! We'll let you know via our socials, chats or another DevLog for details.

ReactOS Italia

We've recently managed to acquire ReactOS Italia's Twitter Account from the previous management with the intention of continuing its reach towards the Italian Community! As such, with it being a part of ReactSquad, we've integrated it into our website, which has a separate page! If you've been in our homepage defaulted at ReactOS-PH, click the IT link to visit the landing page for the Italian Division.

Any more to come?

Right now we're in the process of consolidating every piece that we have missed during the development, including the recent ReactOS Tests! Once we're done, we'll keep you updated on the future roadmap.


Thank you for your undying support for ReactSquad, and we remain committed to providing more to the table that unifies the experience of all the communities with our main goal: To introduce ReactOS, an open-source alternative to Windows!
