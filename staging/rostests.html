ReactOS Tests

in English (Preview)

Please note that this format contains all of the tests that were committed since the Main PR's launch on February 2009, and may be updated to include older tests. All dates are in UTC.




2012

Office 2007 on ReactOS

28 Dec 2012

Microsoft Office 2007 is confirmed to work on ReactOS Nightly (Arwinss ver.)!

Two Microsoft products on ReactOS

27 Dec 2012

GTASAnek published a video on YouTube demonstrating that Microsoft Office 2003 and Windows Media PLayer 11 are working on ReactOS Nightly!

Two apps work on ReactOS Nightly

12 Sep 2012

@mperu99 confirms that UltraVNC 1.0.9.6.2 and TightVNC 2.5.2 works on ReactOS Nightly! The post is preserved for documentation, but the actual image is nowhere to be found.

Trillian 5.1 works on ReactOS Nightly

6 Sep 2012

@vicmarcal confirmed in a forum post that Trillian IM 5.1 is working on ReactOS!

ReactOS Nightly with 64MB RAM

20 Feb 2012

Zefklop has proven that ReactOS Nightly runs with 64MB of RAM! A new milestone has been set: Running the OS under 32MB.

Simon the Sorcerer works on ReactOS v0.3.14

10 Feb 2012

Forum user ArmyMan007 confirms that with the help of ScummVM and sound drivers, Simon the Sorcerer was playable on ReactOS v0.3.14! Also works was HydraIRC.

Firefox 10 runs on ReactOS v0.3.14

9 Feb 2012

Forum user giacomo986 expresses that Firefox 10 is confirmed to work on ReactOS v0.3.14! The Internet connection doesn't seem to be working, however. The forum post is in Italian.

Quake III works on ReactOS Nightly

7 Jan 2012

@Jeditobe (through ReactOSTV on YouTube) has demonstrated action on ReactOS through Quake III. There's no sound and 3D acceleration, however.
